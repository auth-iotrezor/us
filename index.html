Trezor.io/Start® - Starting Up Your Device | Trezor®

To start up your Trezor device, follow these steps:

1. Unbox Your Trezor

  • Open the package and take out your Trezor device, USB cable, and any other included accessories.

2. Connect Your Trezor

  • Connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable.

3. Go to Trezor.io/Start

  • Open your web browser and navigate to Trezor.io/Start.

4. Install Trezor Bridge

  • If prompted, download and install Trezor Bridge, which is a communication tool that allows your Trezor device to interact with your computer.

5. Initialize Your Device

  • Follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your Trezor device. This typically involves:
  • Setting up a new wallet or recovering an existing one.
  • Creating a PIN for your device.
  • Writing down your recovery seed (a series of words that can be used to recover your wallet if you lose access to your Trezor).

6. Secure Your Recovery Seed

  • Write down the recovery seed on the provided recovery card.
  • Store the recovery card in a safe place. Do not share it with anyone or store it digitally.

7. Access Your Wallet

  • Once your device is set up, you can access your wallet through the Trezor web interface.
  • You can now send, receive, and manage your cryptocurrencies securely.

Tips for Security

  • Always double-check the URL to ensure you are on the official Trezor website.
  • Keep your Trezor firmware up to date.
  • Never share your PIN or recovery seed with anyone.
